How to Play

Ludo is a classic board game for 2-4 players. The goal is to move all four of your pieces from your starting area to the center of the board (home).

  • Players take turns rolling a six-sided dice.
  • To move a piece out of the starting area, you must roll a 6.
  • If you roll a 6, you get an extra turn.
  • If your piece lands on a square occupied by an opponent's piece, the opponent's piece is sent back to its starting area.
  • Safe squares (marked with a circle) protect pieces from being captured.
  • The first player to get all four pieces to the center wins!

Controls: Use your mouse to rotate and zoom the 3D view. Click on a piece to select it after rolling the dice.
